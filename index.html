Just the BETA Features

Chat Bot Analytics

#ChatVago brings the power of chat bot analytics to the hands of your clients. You're clients will be able to see how many new leads (first time messages) you generate everyday as well as other key metrices

White Labeled Backend

That's right. #ChatVago is completly white-labled. Choose any domain you want and let your clients login to a completely white-labled backend. Impress your clients with this completed white-labled backend. Now you can charge more for your services!

Works with any Bot Building Platform

Using ManyChat, Chatfuel, or any other bot building platform to build bots for your clients? #ChatVago works with them all. Build on the platform you like most and still give your clients a custom backend.

Allow your clients to send Broadcasts

#ChatVago allows you to let your clients see their subscriber list, analytics, and create custom broadcasts to send to their subscribers. They no longer need to send you the content to broadcast out. They can do it on their own in their white-labeled backend.
