Just the BETA Features

Chat Bot Analytics

#ChatVago brings the power of chat bot analytics to the hands of your clients. You're clients will be able to see how many new leads (first time messages) you generate everyday as well as other key metrices

White Labeled Backend

That's right. #ChatVago is completly white-labled. Choose any domain you want and let your clients login to a completely white-labled backend. Impress your clients with this completed white-labled backend. Now you can charge more for your services!

Works with any Bot Building Platform

Using ManyChat, Chatfuel, or any other bot building platform to build bots for your clients? #ChatVago works with them all. Build on the platform you like most and still give your clients a custom backend.

Allow your clients to send Broadcasts

#ChatVago allows you to let your clients see their subscriber list, analytics, and create custom broadcasts to send to their subscribers. They no longer need to send you the content to broadcast out. They can do it on their own in their white-labeled backend.

#ChatVago Concept and Development Timeline

Tier5 has been developing software for over 5 years as a company and over 100 years collectively. #ChatVago is a new type of software envisioned by Jon Vaughn (CEO of Tier5) and inspired by many chatbot builders. Below you will find the timeline of concept, planning, funding, development, and deployment of #ChatVago Beta! Time Line written in the first person narrative of Jon Vaughn (CEO of Tier5).

  • The problem arises!

    Jan 2018

    The begining of the year I first started realizing this problem. It came to me two fold. On one side I had many clients that had paid me to build chatbots for them. I built some bots on Manychat and others on Chatfuel, others completly custom solutions. My clients would email me wanting to know how the bot was preforming, how many subscribers they had, or asking me if I could send out a broadcast to all of their users. The problem is I didn't want to give them access to manychat or chatfuel. I noticed many people in chatbot groups I'm in expressing the same problems! I looked for a solution, but there was none to be found.

  • Brain Storming a Solution

    Feb 2018

    As time passed and I kept having this same issue and I began to see more and more posts in other chat bot groups about this same issue I really began to brain storm a solution to this problem. I kept looking for a solution in the market and kept coming up empty handed. I started looking through facebook apis, chatbot apis, 3rd party softwares, anything and everything that could help me find a solution. Still empty handed I decided it was time to engineer my own solution. So I took everything I've learned and put together a plan.

  • The ProtoType and Funding

    March 2018

    It took me a few days but I worked out a solution in my head. I took that solution out of my head, put it on paper, and began to plan out the time and cost to bring this solution to life. I pitched the solution to members of my private group, a few of my clients, and some of my good friends looking for funding (didn't want to pay out of my pocket). Most were skeptical that my solution would actually work. So I spent a few days and me and a few of my engineers came up with an actual working proto-type of the solution. Showed the working proto-type to the same group of people and with in a few hours had the full funding I needed to build the Beta Version of #ChatVago

  • Beta Development

    March 2018

    I belive in #ChatVago. I need #ChatVago, and I already know so many of my students need it. So many people have told me they need #ChatVago. I didn't wait for the funding to be in. Before even collecting funding I had already assemblyed a team of 4 engieers at Tier5 and had them start planning and building the Beta Version of #ChatVago. We currently have a team of 5 (out of 37) Tier5 Software Engineers working on the beta version of #ChatVago

  • Beta Release

    April 2018

    I can't wait to use #ChatVago. My students can't wait to use #ChatVago. I've stacked up a team of Tier5 engieers to get a stable beta version of #ChatVago out as soon as possible. We anticipate that by mid or end of April for limited users. The full release of #ChatVago in a full scalable production ready state is anticipated no later than July 1st 2018.
